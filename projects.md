A selection of areas I keep coming back to outside of - and alongside - my day-to-day research work.

**RNA-seq & single-cell analysis**
Ongoing work building and refining analysis pipelines for bulk and single-cell RNA-seq data, focused on transcription factor profiling and cell identity in pancreatic islets.

**Data visualization**
Experiments in creative and custom data visualization, moving beyond default plotting libraries to build figures that communicate a specific analytical point clearly.

**Software development**
Small tools and scripts written to support reproducible bioinformatics workflows - version-controlled, documented, and built to be reused rather than run once.
