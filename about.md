I am a bioinformatics scientist with 7+ years of post-PhD experience providing centralized analytical support to diverse research investigators within a service core environment. I am proficient in end-to-end analytical workflows spanning transcriptomics at multiple resolutions (bulk, single-cell, and spatial), applying machine learning methods to complex biological datasets. I have had the privilege of collaborating with principal investigators, physicians, experimental scientists, graduate students, and computational teams, adapting and implementing new computational tools to support diverse research goals. I value reproducible, transparent, and well-documented research practices that promote rigor, collaboration, and the long-term usability of computational analyses. I also have a growing interest in integrating genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, and epigenomics data to advance clinical bioinformatics, precision medicine, and the discovery of biologically meaningful insights across multiple molecular layers.
